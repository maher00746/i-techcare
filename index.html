Why iTechCare


In all our projects, we communicate data with our customers professionally. We first receive the projects requests, then we send back our analytical study to make sure that we have well understood our customer’s requirements. Upon its confirmation, we proceed to do more detailed study, and submit it to the customer again. Once we have made sure that we are on the right way, the programming phase starts and the first version will be put under test. Upon the end of the testing phase, and once we get our customer satisfaction, we proceed to the handover. After sale support is provided for free to our customers. Maintenance phase will be planned upon the request. Future expansions and modification are guaranteed by our team. Therefore, our customers’ satisfaction will not only be for the whole project, but it will be ensured phase by phase too.
